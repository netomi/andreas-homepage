Curriculum Vitae

german version

Andreas Pöttler was born in 1985 in Graz, and began his musical training in Pöllau at the age of six. During his time there, he won several prizes at various national “Prima La Musica” youth competitions.

In 2004, he completed the preparatory course for guitar at the University for Music and Performing Arts in Vienna. To pursue further study in the guitar, he enrolled a year later at the Private University Konservatorium Wien.

Andreas graduated with honors and received a Master’s degree of Arts in 2012. In addition, he has a Master's degree in Music Education (Voice and Instrumental) from the University of Music and Performing Arts Vienna.

During his studies, he received several scholarships from the BMWF, as well as a grant from the TOKYO FOUNDATION in 2012. In the same year, he won the “High Virtuosity” competition, which enabled him to debut as a soloist in the Wiener Musikverein.

His love for education has led to a teaching position at the Weiz School of Music. Additionally, he is a welcomed lecturer at various seminars. He has recently founded his own jazz/pop band, where he is the main songwriter.

Highlights of Andreas’ musical career:

  • TV appearance for BIA TV (Hungary)
  • Radio recordings for Ö1, Radio Steiermark and Radio Soundportal
  • Guest musician at the Murcia Youth Symphonieorchester (Spain) in 2008,
    at the Wiener Jeunesse Orchester and the Webern Symphonieorchester in 2009
  • Numerous national and international solo concerts in Austria, Germany, Czech Republic, Poland, Hungary, and Canada.
